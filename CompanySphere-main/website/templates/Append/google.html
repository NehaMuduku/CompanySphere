The GOOGLE

LOGO

The colors used in the Google logo each have their own significance and together they convey a sense of creativity, innovation, and playfulness.

  • Blue: The color blue is used for the "G" and the second "g" in "Google," emphasizing the company's foundation and stability.
  • Red: The color red is used for the first "o" and the "e" in "Google," drawing attention and adding a vibrant energy to the logo.
  • Yellow: The color yellow is used for the second "o" in "Google," suggesting creativity and a positive, welcoming presence.
  • Green: The color green is used for the "l" in "Google," breaking the primary color pattern and symbolizing the company's willingness to innovate and think outside the box.
