The WIPRO

LOGO

Wipro Limited, an Indian multinational corporation providing information technology, consulting, and business process services, has a logo that reflects its values, growth, and evolution over the years.

  • Wings Symbol: The prominent feature of the Wipro logo is the stylized "W" with wings. These wings symbolize innovation, progress, and the company's ambition to soar to greater heights in the global marketplace.
  • Bold Font: The font used for "Wipro" in the logo is bold and modern, reflecting strength, reliability, and leadership in the IT services and consulting sector.
  • Color Scheme: The logo typically uses a combination of green and blue colors. Green symbolizes growth, harmony, and sustainability, while blue signifies trust, professionalism, and technology.
